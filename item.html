    DETAILS

    Festool Limited-Edition DOMINO DF 500, Accessories, and Tenon Kit 578509

    Often, even just a millisecond is crucial: The kickback is the most common cause of serious injuries when using a track saw. Our unique kickback stop therefore stops the saw blade of our cordless track saw in the blink of an eye, and so not only protects your workpiece but also helps to minimize the risk of injury to your hands. In addition, this now allows users to make powerful, precise cuts at up to twice the speed with an extended battery range. When combined, our new generation of saw blades allows for maximum cutting performance and perfect cutting quality. The sawing system is rounded off with clever system accessories, such as guide rails, an angle stop, splinter guard and saw table.

     

    Features:

    • The kickback stop helps to reduce the risk of injury from kickback when sawing or plunging the tool into the workpiece
    • Concentrated torque and up to twice as fast work progress with an increased saw blade service life
    • Brushless EC-TEC motor combined with lithium-ion batteries for maximum cutting power
    • Low-dust mobile working thanks to dust collection bag or an extractor with Bluetooth® automatic switch-on
    • The kickback stop can be deactivated for individual cuts
    • Top-class sawing system thanks to a variety of accessories

     

    Main application:

    • Precise circular in materials up to 2-1/8" (55 mm) thick
    • Cutting interior doors to length using guide rail and circular saw
    • Cutting recesses in doors and kitchen worktops
    • Manufacturing expansion joints in parquet flooring and false joints
    • Cutting chipboard to size

     

    Specifications:

    • Battery voltage: 2 x 18 V
    • Idle engine speed: 2650 - 3800/5200 RPM
    • Battery capacity: 5.00 Ah
    • Drive type: Battery
    • Technology: Bluetooth®
    • Cutting depth at 45°: 1-11/16” (43 mm)
    • Saw blade diameter: 6-1/4" (160 mm)
    • Weight: 7.94 lbs (3.6 kg)
    • Dust extraction connection dia.: 1-1/16"/1-7/16" (27/36 mm)
    • Cutting depth: 0" - 2-1/8” (0 - 55 mm)
    • Angular range: -1 - 47 °
